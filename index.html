About Me

Hi, I'm Cole DenBleyker, a second-year Software Engineering student at RIT!
I am passionate about developing, planning, and maintaining code. Growing up, I was interested in solving puzzles, being passionate about technology, and how things are made which all came together when I helped my dad build a new family computer in elementary school when our old one died. After taking a few computer science courses in high school, I fell in love with programming, which was the combination of everything I was interested in! I was making applications from the ground up, there are several hundreds if not thousands of ways to “solve” the puzzle that would get my program to meet the desired requirements. When it was time to enroll and choose a major at RIT, I knew I wanted to be a Software Engineer.
